Blue Ocean Sciences Media Education (BOS ME)

Bringing Science to Life

Through a dynamic media alliance, Blue Ocean Sciences Media Education program, (BOS ME) , the world will be able to follow this media savvy team of researchers daily, as they introduce complex scientific subjects to the world through fun easy-to-understand webisodes, blogs, and online question and answer sessions all displayed on the WAVES portion of the BOS interactive website - Web Assisted Virtual Education Sustainability .

The BOS Me media group consist of Blue Ocean Productions, Cage Free Productions, Highliner Studios and Blue Ocean Sciences (BOS). Mentored by Dr. Dijanna Figueroa, narrator of James Cameron’s “Aliens of The Deep”, and by BOS President Dr. Andrea Neal, the award-winning environmental entrepreneur class from Santa Barbara High School’s Don’s Net Café will introduce the Marine Debris Dream Team. Students will interview these high level researchers prior to and upon return from the expedition.
