Blue Ocean Sciences Synthesis Alliance(BOSSA)

The primary goal of BOSSA is to foster scientific collaborations through the sharing of scientists, facilities, information, media, educational programs, and technologies. BOSSA will develop: large accessible databases of information, new equipment for monitoring and for remote sensing systems, and innovative solutions for environmental contaminants.
