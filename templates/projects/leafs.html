Living Environmental Adaptable Floating Structures(LEAFS)

Partnering with Life Cube, www.lifecubeinc.com, the primary focuses for the LEAFS program will be environmentally sustainable disaster relief. LEAFS offers the perfect solution with a floating desalination greenhouse. This structure offers a unique balance between portability, functionality, and price. Due to rising sea levels caused by climate change, sustainable agriculture solutions need to be found and developed before it is too late.
